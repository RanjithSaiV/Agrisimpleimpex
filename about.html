Abut Us

Agri Simple Impex Private limited increased operations beginning in 2024 and Expanding to GULF countries . With 6 years of experience in the domestic market, we are supplying quality Coconuts and Bananas to both the domestic market and a major supermarket chain in India.

Infrastructure

We run a BRC-accredited pack house with a contemporary pack house and cold storage facility for Bananas, Tender Coconuts, . In the heart of the farming region.

Quality

Being a quality-conscious company, we continuously monitor each of our linked growers' products from the time they begin to grow until they are packaged. If necessary, we can also have the products tested in APEDA recognized laboratories.

Packing

Packing is carried out in APEDA board Approved pack house in hygienic packaging material to suit end users requirements for export as well as domestic market..

How We Works

Agri Simple Impex Private limited works with several Global GAP growers and offers them techno- managerial help from the start of the crop. We have a dedicated team of professionals who communicate with all producers on a regular basis about crop management, certifications, food safety, and quality assurance. Our guiding concept is to use our extensive experience to offer all clients the best products and services possible.

Mission

Quality is not an accident, a miracle, or something that is presumed; rather, it is the result of consistent, long-term efforts with great managerial and technological aptitude. We are devoted to upholding our strong privilege of satisfying the needs of our cherished clients, fostering their confidence, and enhancing the prosperity of growers.

FOOD SAFETY

Agri Simple Impex Private limited has developed a series of guidelines to make sure all of our farmers follow good agricultural practises (G.A.P.) The most modern production and handling techniques are among Agri Simple's most innovative agricultural methods. We strictly regulate the use of agrochemicals and collaborate with the "Food Chain Partnership." Samples are frequently sent to an approved laboratory for examination.
